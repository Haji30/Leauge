BANDLE CITY

Bandle City is a whimsical and magical realm hidden within the forests of Runeterra. It is the home of the yordles, small, playful creatures who possess a strong affinity for magic and nature. The city is characterized by its vibrant colors, peculiar architecture, and an ever-changing landscape that reflects the imagination of its inhabitants. Bandle City is known for its carefree spirit, creativity, and connection to the spirit of the forest. The yordles often engage in playful antics and adventures, using their magic to protect their home from external threats. Notable champions from Bandle City include Teemo, the swift scout known for his guerrilla tactics and poisonous traps; Lulu, a whimsical mage who can manipulate the world around her and transform her allies; and Rumble, a clever inventor who pilots a mech to fight for his friends.

DEMACIA

Demacia is a proud and noble kingdom founded on principles of justice, honor, and duty. Its citizens are known for their strong sense of morality and commitment to protecting the innocent. The kingdom is characterized by its grand castles, lush landscapes, and a well-organized military. Demacia values order and righteousness, often viewing magic with suspicion, especially if it threatens their ideals. The kingdom is known for its knights and champions who uphold justice and fight against tyranny. Notable champions from Demacia include Garen, a stalwart warrior and symbol of Demacian might; Lux, a powerful mage who uses her light-based magic to protect her homeland; and Jarvan IV, the prince of Demacia, known for his leadership and valor in battle.

IONIA

Ionia is a mystical land filled with ancient traditions, spiritual practices, and a deep connection to nature. The kingdom is home to various martial arts and philosophies that emphasize harmony with the natural world. Ionia has faced external threats but remains resilient in preserving its culture. Ionia values peace, balance, and the protection of its natural surroundings. The people are often spiritual and practice various forms of martial arts that are deeply rooted in their beliefs. Notable champions from Ionia include Zed, a master of shadow manipulation and a skilled assassin; Kennen, a yordle ninja who harnesses the power of lightning; and Irelia, a skilled blade dancer who fights to protect her homeland.

NOXUS

Noxus is a ruthless and expansionist empire that values strength and ambition above all else. It is characterized by its militaristic society, where the strong rise to power and the weak are often subjugated. The kingdom is known for its brutal conquests and the use of dark magic. Noxus is a land where power is the ultimate currency, and ambition is encouraged. Its citizens are often hardened warriors and cunning strategists who will do anything to achieve their goals. Notable champions from Noxus include Darius, a fearsome general known for his brutal strength and leadership; Swain, the cunning leader of Noxus, who uses dark magic to manipulate his enemies; and Katarina, a deadly assassin skilled in the art of combat and subterfuge.

PILTOVER

Piltover is a city of innovation and progress, known as the "City of Progress." It is a hub of technological advancement, where inventors and scientists push the boundaries of what is possible. The city thrives on trade and invention, making it a beacon of opportunity. Piltover is characterized by its towering spires, bustling markets, and a culture that celebrates ingenuity. The citizens are often driven by ambition and a desire to improve the world through technology. Notable champions from Piltover include Jayce, a brilliant inventor and warrior who wields a transforming hammer; Caitlyn, the skilled sheriff of Piltover, known for her marksmanship; and Heimerdinger, a genius yordle scientist who creates advanced technology and turrets.

ZAUN

Zaun is the underbelly of Piltover, characterized by pollution, crime, and a lack of regulation. It is a city where the poor and marginalized struggle to survive amidst the chaos. Despite its challenges, Zaun is a place of opportunity for those who are willing to take risks and push the boundaries of what is possible. Zaun values cunning and resourcefulness, and its citizens are often driven by a desire to survive and thrive in a harsh environment. Notable champions from Zaun include Janna, a skilled smuggler and wind mage; Twisted Fate, a cunning card shark and master of probability; and Dr. Mundo, a brilliant scientist who has dedicated his life to the pursuit of knowledge and power.
